Background

Ebenezer Malomo & Company is a firm of Chartered Accountants, which provides Auditing, Taxation, Accountancy and Management Consultancy Services to Individuals, Corporate Bodies, Government and Non-Governmental Organizations. The firm is also a registered Reporting Accountant with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). The firm consists of well-experienced professionals with a wide range of multi-disciplinary background. The diverse nature of the firm enables it to provide independent impartial, comprehensive management support, audit assurance and investigative services. Others are taxation and financial advisory services based on high level of professional competence.
